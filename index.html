Our Services

We offer a range of classes and one‑to‑one sessions designed to meet the needs of puppies, adult dogs and families.

Puppy Classes (8‑20 weeks)

Fun, reward‑based group classes that teach basic commands, socialisation and confidence building.

Highlights: max 4 pups per class, 90 min sessions, free starter manual.

Adult Dog Classes

Ideal for dogs that have completed puppy work or need a refresher on leash manners, recall and impulse control.

Highlights: same small‑group format, optional 1‑to‑1 follow‑up.

One‑to‑One (121) Sessions

Tailored home visits where Emma works directly with you and your dog to target specific behaviours.

Duration: 60 min (can be extended on request).

Behaviour Support

Professional help for reactivity, excessive barking, separation anxiety or any challenge that needs a personalised plan.

Method: Positive reinforcement, clicker training and practical homework.

Workshops & Scent Classes

Special one‑off workshops covering recall, lead‑walking, “Improvers” tricks, and a fun scent‑work introduction for all ages.

Kids‑Around‑Dogs Programme

Designed for families – teaching children how to safely interact with dogs, read body language and build confidence.

Run quarterly in our Harpenden community hall.

Course Curriculum

Basic Obedience (6 weeks)

Week 1 – Sit, Stay, come when called.
Week 2 – Loose leash walking.
Week 3 – Leave it & Drop it.
Week 4 – Recall in low‑distraction environments.
Week 5 – Gentle greetings with people.
Week 6 – Final assessment & graduation.

£120 / 6 weeks

Socialisation (4 weeks)

Weekly play‑dates with other puppies, exposure to traffic, shopping centres, public transport and a variety of sounds.

£100 / 4 weeks

Advanced Tricks (8 weeks)

Fun, eye‑catching tricks – “Shake”, “Spin”, “Roll over”, “Play dead” and “Fetch specific toys”.

£150 / 8 weeks

All courses finish with a small graduation ceremony where each pup receives a personalised rosette, a copy of the Puppy School manual and a bag of Natures Menu treats.
