Frequently Asked Questions

What age should my puppy be to join?

We accept puppies from 8 weeks up to 20 weeks (or a little older if the vet clears them).

Do I need to bring my own treats?

All treats used during class are provided. You may bring a favourite snack for home practice.

How long is each session?

Each class runs for 90 minutes, once per week.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 48 hours notice; otherwise the full class fee applies.

Do you offer private 1‑to‑1 sessions?

Yes – the “121” service can be booked via the “Events” section or by contacting us directly.

Can I bring my own dog to the first class?

All puppies must be up‑to‑date on vaccinations and cleared by a vet before attending.
