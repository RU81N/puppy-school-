About Puppy School Harpenden

Founded by Emma Green, a certified ABTC‑registered trainer with over ten years of experience, Puppy School Harpenden offers a warm, supportive environment where puppies learn the foundations of good behaviour through positive, reward‑based methods.

Our Mission

To raise confident, well‑behaved dogs that are a joy to live with – for both the puppy and its human family.

Why Choose Us?

  • Small class size – max 4 puppies
  • Certified trainer (ABTC)
  • Positive reinforcement only
  • Flexible weekday & weekend sessions
  • Free Puppy School manual and welcome treats

How It Works – From Pup to Pro

1️⃣ Enrol

Book a place online or over the phone – we’ll send a welcome pack straight to your inbox.

2️⃣ Attend

Weekly 90‑minute sessions, built on fun games, treats and clear cues.

3️⃣ Practice

Take home a printable worksheet each week – short daily sessions keep progress fast.

4️⃣ Graduate

Each pup receives a graduation rosette, a copy of the manual and a bag of Natures Menu treats.

Our Services

We offer a range of classes and one‑to‑one sessions designed to meet the needs of puppies, adult dogs and families.

Puppy Classes (8‑20 weeks)

Fun, reward‑based group classes teaching basic commands, socialisation and confidence building. Max 4 pups per class.

Adult Dog Classes

Ideal for dogs that have completed puppy work or need a refresher on leash manners, recall and impulse control.

One‑to‑One (121) Sessions

Tailored home visits where Emma works directly with you and your dog to target specific behaviours. 60 min per session.

Behaviour Support

Professional help for reactivity, excessive barking, separation anxiety, or any challenge that needs a personalised plan.

Workshops & Scent Classes

Special one‑off workshops covering recall, lead‑walking, “Improvers” tricks, and a fun scent‑work introduction for all ages.

Kids‑Around‑Dogs Programme

Designed for families – teaching children how to safely interact with dogs, read body language and build confidence. Runs quarterly.

Course Curriculum (6‑Week Basic Obedience)

Week 1 – Sit & Stay

Foundation commands, focus games, short play breaks.

Week 2 – Loose Leash Walking

Gentle guidance, “heel” exercise, reward timing.

Week 3 – Recall & “Leave It”

Building a reliable “come”, teaching impulse control.

Week 4 – Manners Around People

Polite greetings, sit‑stay at the door, calm walking past strangers.

Week 5 – “Drop It” & “Wait”

Teaching release commands, building patience.

Week 6 – Graduation & Review

Final assessment, rosette ceremony, take‑home manual.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age should my puppy be to join?

We accept puppies from 8 weeks up to 20 weeks (or a little older if the vet clears them).

Do I need to bring my own treats?

All treats used during class are provided. You may bring a favourite snack for home practice.

How long is each session?

Each class runs for 90 minutes, once per week.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 48 hours notice; otherwise the full class fee applies.

Can I book a private 1‑to‑1 session?

Yes – use the “Workshops” section to schedule a Calendly 121 session.

Are vaccinations required?

All puppies must be up‑to‑date on core vaccinations and cleared by a vet before attending.

Do you offer a ‘Puppy Club’ membership?

Every enrolment gives free access to our exclusive Puppy Club online resource hub.

What makes your training different?

We use only positive reinforcement, run tiny class sizes, and provide a free manual and treats with every enrolment.

How many puppies are in each class?

Maximum 4 puppies per class – this ensures plenty of individual attention.

Do you accept walk‑ins?

We prefer pre‑booking to guarantee a place, but you can call us to check availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer, cash, and contactless card payments are all accepted.

Is there a trial class before I commit?

Yes – the first class is completely free, no obligation to continue.

What if my puppy has special needs?

Our trainers are experienced with anxiety, shyness and mild behavioural issues – we’ll adapt the curriculum.

Do you provide a graduate certificate?

Every puppy receives a graduation rosette and a printed manual as a keepsake.
