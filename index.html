Frequently Asked Questions

What age should my puppy be to join?

We accept puppies from 8 weeks up to 20 weeks – as soon as they’re cleared by a vet.

Do I need to bring my own treats?

We provide all treats during class, but you can bring a favourite snack for home practice.

How long is each class?

Each session runs for 90 minutes, once per week.

Is there a cancellation policy?

We ask for 48 hours notice; otherwise the full class fee applies.
